💡 WHEN TO USE DELETE vs DEACTIVATE
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

USE DEACTIVATE WHEN:
  ✅ Product temporarily out of stock
  ✅ Might restock later
  ✅ Want to preserve product history
  ✅ Unsure about permanent removal
  Result: Product hidden, can be reactivated

USE DELETE WHEN:
  ✅ Product permanently discontinued
  ✅ Product no longer available anywhere
  ✅ Duplicate product entry
  ✅ Wrong product added by mistake
  Result: Product completely removed, cannot restore

📞 COMMON QUESTIONS
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q: Can I undo a deletion?
A: No, deletion is permanent. Use Deactivate if you might change your mind.

Q: What about order history?
A: Orders remain in system, just the product reference is removed.
   You can still see "Order #123 on March 20, 2026" in order history.

Q: What if product is in multiple carts?
A: All cart entries deleted automatically.
   All affected users have product removed from their carts.

Q: Does this affect inventory?
A: Yes, the product is removed completely.
   Use Deactivate to preserve inventory history.

Q: Can regular users delete products?
A: No, only administrators can delete products.

Q: Is the image file actually deleted?
A: Yes, files in /uploads/products/ are permanently deleted.
   External URL images are not affected.

Q: How long does deletion take?
A: Usually < 500ms. Page reloads with success message.

Q: What if deletion fails?
A: Page stays on products list, error message shown.
   Check PHP error logs for details.
