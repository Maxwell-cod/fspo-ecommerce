## 🎯 Content Optimization Framework

### **1. Page Titles (SEO Title)**

**Formula**: `[Primary Keyword] + [Secondary Benefit] – [Brand Name]`

**Examples**:
```
❌ Bad:    "Products"
✅ Good:   "High-Quality LED Panel Lights 9W – FSPO Ltd Rwanda"
✅ Better: "Energy-Saving LED Panel Lights 9W | Best Price in Kigali – FSPO Ltd"
```

**Rules**:
- 50-60 characters (fits in Google search result)
- Include primary keyword (1-2 times)
- Include brand name
- Make it compelling (people want to click)
- Unique for every page

**Impact**: 30% CTR improvement possible

---

### **2. Meta Descriptions**

**Formula**: `[What is it] + [Key Benefit] + [Call to Action]`

**Examples**:
```
❌ Bad: "Water heater for sale"

✅ Good: "Premium 130L Electric Water Heater with 5-year warranty. Save energy with thermostat control. Free delivery in Kigali. Shop now!"

✅ Better: "Need hot water? Our 130L electric water heater saves 40% energy. Durable brass fittings, 5-year warranty, fast installation. Order today!"
```

**Rules**:
- 150-160 characters (fits on mobile)
- Include primary keyword naturally
- Mention key benefit/unique selling point
- Include call-to-action (Shop, Buy, Learn, etc.)
- Make people want to click
- Unique for every page

**Impact**: 20-40% CTR improvement

### **3. Product Descriptions**

**Minimum**: 200 words per product  
**Better**: 300-500 words  
**Best**: 500-800 words (competitive keywords)

**Structure**:
```
1. Opening (What is it?)
2. Key Features (3-5 points)
3. Benefits (How does it help?)
4. Specifications (Technical details)
5. Use Cases (When to use?)
6. Call-to-Action (Buy now, Add to cart)
```

```

What is it?
High-quality 9mm gypsum board designed for interior walls and ceilings. 
Perfect for homes, offices, and commercial spaces in Rwanda.

Key Features:
✓ 9mm thickness - ideal wall thickness
✓ Fire-resistant - tested and certified
✓ Lightweight - easy installation
✓ Moisture-resistant - handles Kigali humidity
✓ Cost-effective - great value

Benefits:
- Quick installation = save time and money
- Fire-resistant = protect your family
- Lightweight = reduce construction costs
- Smooth finish = professional look

Specifications:
- Size: 1200mm x 2400mm

- Weight: ~20kg per sheet
- Fire Rating: Type A
- Moisture Content: 10-12%

Uses:

acoustic walls, interior renovation projects

Why Choose Our Gypsum Board?
- Rwanda's trusted supplier since 2015

- Competitive pricing
```

**SEO Tips for Product Descriptions**:
- Include primary keyword in first 100 words
- Use secondary keywords naturally (2-3 times)
- Include long-tail keywords naturally
- Answer common customer questions
- Use bullet points for readability
- Include specifications (good for featured snippets)

### **5. Keyword Research & Placement**

**How to Find Keywords**:

1. **Google Suggestions**
   - Type in Google search
   - See autocomplete suggestions
   - These are real searches people do

2. **Google Search Console**
   - Free from Google
   - Shows what keywords bring traffic

3. **Keyword Tools** (Free)
   - Ubersuggest: https://ubersuggest.com/

4. **Competitor Analysis**
   - See what competitors rank for

**Keyword Placement (for 300-word article)**:
- Primary keyword: 1-3 times (naturally)
- Secondary keywords: 2-3 times
- Related keywords: 5-10 times naturally
- **Don't stuff keywords** - sounds unnatural

**Proper Structure**:
```html
<h1>Main Topic (Page Title) - Only ONE per page</h1>

<h2>Main Section</h2>
<p>Content...</p>

<h3>Subsection</h3>
<p>Content...</p>

<h3>Another Subsection</h3>
<p>Content...</p>

<h2>Another Main Section</h2>
<p>Content...</p>
```

**Example**:
```html
<h1>LED Panel Lights 9W - Energy Efficient Ceiling Lights in Rwanda</h1>
```

### **7. Internal Linking Strategy**

**What**: Link from one page to another related page on your site

**Why**: Helps Google understand site structure, passes SEO value

**How**:

```html
<!-- In product descriptions -->
<p>This <a href="LED-bulbs.html">energy-efficient LED bulb</a> 
   works perfectly with our panel lights.</p>

<!-- In category pages -->
<p>Browse our full <a href="light-fixtures.html">lighting 
   fixtures collection</a>.</p>

<!-- Related products -->
<p>You might also like: 
<a href="hanging-lights.html">Hanging lights</a> or 
<a href="led-bulbs.html">LED bulbs</a>.</p>
```

**Best Practices**:
- Link with descriptive anchor text (not "click here")
- 2-3 internal links per 300-word article
- Link to relevant pages
- Link to important pages more often

### **8. Visual Content Optimization**

**Images**:
```html
<!-- BAD -->
<img src="image1.jpg">

<!-- GOOD -->
<img src="led-panel-light-9w-square.jpg" 
     alt="9W LED Panel Light - Square Recessed Ceiling Mount">
```

**Rules**:
- Descriptive filename (not "image1.jpg")
- Include alt text (helps Google understand image)
- Alt text = what you'd say to someone who can't see it
- Keep file size small (< 100KB for web)
- Use relevant file formats (JPG for photos, PNG for graphics)

**Alt Text Examples**:
```
❌ Bad: "product"
✅ Good: "9W LED Panel Light - Square Recessed Ceiling Mount with Warm White"
✅ Better: "Energy-saving 9W LED panel light with recessed ceiling mount"
```

**CTA Best Practices**:
- Use action words (Add, Buy, Explore, Learn, etc.)
- Create urgency ("Limited Stock", "Free Today Only")
- Make it stand out (color, size, position)
- Place above the fold (visible without scrolling)

## 🎯 Content Strategy by Page Type

### **Homepage**
- What: Brief intro + key categories + unique selling points
- Keywords: Brand name + main category keywords
- Length: 200-400 words
- Links: To main categories, featured products

### **Category Pages**
- What: Category overview + product filtering + category description
- Keywords: Category name + location (e.g., "Building Materials Rwanda")
- Length: 250-500 words
- Links: To products in category + related categories

### **Product Pages**
- What: Complete product info + specifications + reviews
- Keywords: Product name + benefits + materials
- Length: 300-800 words (higher for competitive products)
- Links: To related products + category page

### **Blog/Article**
- What: In-depth, helpful information on topic
- Keywords: Long-tail, question-based keywords
- Length: 1000-3000 words minimum
- Links: To product pages, other articles

## 💡 Content Writing Tips

### **Write for Humans, Optimize for Search**

❌ **Bad** (keyword-stuffed):
```
"Best LED lights Rwanda. LED lights in Rwanda. Buy LED lights Rwanda. 
LED lights price Rwanda. LED lights Kigali. Quality LED lights Rwanda."
```

✅ **Good** (natural writing):
```
"Looking for energy-efficient LED lights in Rwanda? FSPO Ltd offers 
high-quality LED solutions for homes and businesses across Kigali. 
Our LED lights save up to 80% energy compared to traditional bulbs."
```

### **Answer Questions**

Think about what customers ask:
- "How much does it cost?"
- "Where can I buy?"
- "How long does it last?"
- "Is it good quality?"
- "How do I install it?"

Answer these directly in your content.

### **Use Numbers**

People love specific numbers:
```
✅ "Save 80% on energy costs"
✅ "5-year warranty"
✅ "Delivered in 24 hours"
✅ "Over 1000 happy customers"
```

### **Include Social Proof**

- Customer testimonials
- Review ratings
- Case studies
- Success stories
